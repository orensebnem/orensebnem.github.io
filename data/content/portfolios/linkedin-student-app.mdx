# Background

[Linkedin](https://linkedin.com/) is full of job opportunities 
for any level of positions. Our corporate customers complain for not 
reaching our right applicants for entry level positions

The new graduates are pretty overwhelmed 
and frustrated with being inexperienced 
in job seeking.

__What new grads need is more than a jobs directory.__
Our customised solution for Students will provide a full support to the new graduates in their job seeking journey

# Business Case

## Initial Focus

* 4 millions of graduates every year only in the U.S. (Source: [Statista](https://statista.com))
* 3.4 millions of entry level positions in the U.S.  (Source: [Statista](https://statista.com))
* Recruiters suffer from finding the right candidate for the jobs
* The destructive effect of outstanding student loan debt on economics (around $1.6 Trillion) (Source: [Forbes](https://forbes.com))

__College students need professional help in their job search__
* A guide to tell them what they are able to do 
* A trainer to teach them how to present their skills
